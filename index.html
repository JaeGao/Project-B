Double Jump

Game Controls

  • Move Left: A
  • Move Right: D
  • Jump: W
  • Crouch: S

Background

Double Jump is a thrilling platformer game that combines the excitement of jumping and the creativity of platform generation. In this game, one player takes on the role of the character, navigating through a dynamically generated world, while the other player uses a camera to create platforms in real-time.

The objective of the game is to survive as long as possible by collecting coins and avoiding falling into the rising lava. The player controlling the character must skillfully jump and crouch to overcome obstacles and reach higher grounds. Meanwhile, the player using the camera strategically creates platforms by capturing white objects, providing a path for the character to traverse.

With each coin collected, the player earns points, and the goal is to accumulate the highest score possible within the given time limit. The game features stunning visuals, immersive sound effects, and a captivating gameplay experience that keeps players engaged and challenged.

Double Jump offers a unique collaborative gaming experience where teamwork and quick reflexes are essential for success. Can you and your partner work together to survive the ever-changing landscape and achieve the ultimate high score? Get ready to jump, crouch, and create in this exhilarating platformer!
